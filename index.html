The real cost of manual operations

Every day you wait costs you money you can count.

Most clinics in Cameroon lose 20–40% of revenue to operational gaps they don't even know they have. This is where it goes.

🧾
Billing that never gets recorded
Services rendered, drugs dispensed, labs run — but the patient is discharged before the invoice is complete. The money disappears and there's no paper trail.
↓ 15–25% revenue lost per day
💊
Drugs that walk out the door
Without batch tracking and dispensing records, controlled substances and high-value medications disappear between the shelf and the patient. No one notices until stock-take.
↓ Average 12% pharmacy shrinkage
📂
Patient files that vanish
A returning patient. A history of hypertension and ACE-inhibitor allergy. The paper file was filed under the wrong date. The doctor prescribes without the history. The patient pays the price.
↓ 60% of files misfiled or lost
🔬
Lab results that never reach the doctor
Results written on a slip, handed to the patient, sometimes remembered. The STAT malaria RDT from 9am is still sitting on the bench at 4pm. No one followed up. The patient was discharged.
↓ 30% of critical results delayed
📊
Management decisions made blind
You can't tell which service makes the most money. You don't know which doctor is overbooked. Your top-selling drug is always out of stock because reorder points are in someone's head.
↓ Zero visibility = poor decisions
🏥
Multi-location chaos
You open a second branch. Now you have two sets of books, two WhatsApp groups, two inventory systems, and no consolidated picture of what the business is actually doing.
↓ Expansion multiplies every problem

Fraud & revenue protection

Know exactly what happened. Who did it. When.

Every action in TotalCare OS is timestamped and attributed to a specific staff member. The ledger is immutable. Stock movements are reconciled automatically.

📋
Complete audit trail
Every dispensing, every payment, every edit — logged with timestamp and staff name. No one can delete entries.
🔐
Role-level permission control
Define exactly what each role can see and do. Permissions enforced at database level — not just hidden in the UI.
📦
Inventory reconciliation
Stock decremented on dispensing, incremented on receiving. Variance between physical count and system is visible immediately.
💳
Immutable financial ledger
Payments are posted, not edited. Every adjustment creates a new line. No one can change a payment after the fact — including the owner.
